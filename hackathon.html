Web Development
Web development is the work involved in developing a web site for the Internet or an intranet ( private network). It can range from developing a simple single static page of plain text to complex web-based internet applications (web apps), electronic businesses, and social network services. Therefore, we encourage you to participate in this hackathon event and horn your skills in this field, learn from the very basics and end up building your own project.
App Development
Mobile app development is the process by which a mobile app is developed, such as personal digital assistants, enterprise digital assistants or mobile phones. These applications can be pre-installed on phones during manufacturing platforms, or delivered as web applications using server-side or client-side processing to provide an "application-like" experience within a Web browser. With BeingArtifex learn AppD through a project - based approach.
Graphic Design
Graphic design is a craft where professionals create visual content to communicate messages. By applying visual hierarchy and page layout techniques, designers use typography and pictures to meet users' specific needs and focus on the logic of displaying elements in interactive designs, to optimize the user experience. BeingArtifex have provided you the best way to learn this in a competitive manner by participating in this hackathon.
Coding
A revolutionary communication interface developed by humans for interacting with computers through instructions is the skill of coding. It has laid out the modern framework of a digitalized society by developing the technological landscape. We invite you to participate in our Hackathon and showcase your coding skills on a competitive forum.
Robotics
The interdisciplinary domain of science, engineering and technology rendering an opportunity for replacing humans with programmed machines to assist and explore beyond our reach is the ever-evolving field of robotics. We have our doors open at Hackathon for students interested in programming and designing machines to replicate human actions and widen our reach.
